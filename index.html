MEMBERSHIP EXAMINATION

S Competence exams are conducted for candidates who have a dream of starting their own business or company & seeking self employment opportunity within India. Those interested aspirants can crack the exam and avail Certification & membership with proper Funding & Support in order to setup their Dream Business. Exams are in the form of Online Aptitude Test contains Level I & Level II.

Level I is a Home Proctored Online Assessment, candidate can take the exams from their Remote Location. Those who crack Level I exams are eligible to take Final Benchmark Exam as Level II.
